# 🎬 Project 1 — AI Video Language Transformation

## 🎯 Objective

Transform **three existing Chinese-language instructional videos** into:

* An **English audio version**, or, 
* A **French audio version**,

Subtitles (CN / EN / FR / bilingual) may also be added as an enhancement.

The goal is to leverage AI tools to produce a high-quality multilingual video transformation pipeline.

## 🧠 Task Description

You are free to design your own AI pipeline. A typical workflow may include:

1. Extracting the original Chinese audio
2. Transcribing the speech (if needed)
3. Translating the content into English and/or French
4. Generating synthetic speech
5. Replacing the original audio track
6. Producing a synchronized final video

You are **not required** to follow these exact steps, as long as the final output satisfies the following:

* Clear audio
* Accurate and coherent translation
* Proper synchronization
* A watchable and technically consistent final video

The implementation format is flexible.

---

## 🔧 Tools

You may use any AI tools, including:

* Speech-to-text (ASR) models
* Machine translation systems
* Text-to-speech (TTS) systems
* Voice cloning models
* AI video editing software
* Cloud-based AI services
* Open-source frameworks

There are **no strict tool restrictions** for this project.

## 📊 Evaluation Criteria (15 Points)

To receive strong scores:

* The output video must be complete
* Audio must be understandable
* Translation must be reasonably accurate
* Audio-video synchronization must be coherent
* The final rendering must be functional and professional

Creativity, improved voice quality, better synchronization, or more refined system design may positively influence your evaluation.
